How much does professional lawn care cost in Waco?

Lawn care costs in Waco typically range from $30-150 per visit depending on property size and services. M&M Lawn Service provides free quotes with transparent pricing. Factors include lawn size, service frequency, terrain complexity, and additional services like fertilization.

How often should I have my lawn mowed in Waco?

Most Waco lawns need mowing weekly during growing season (March-October) and bi-weekly in winter. Texas heat and grass types like Bermuda and St. Augustine grow rapidly in spring/summer, requiring consistent maintenance for optimal health and appearance.
