Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed in Waco?

During growing season (April-October), weekly mowing is ideal for most Waco lawns. In winter months, bi-weekly or monthly service may be sufficient depending on grass type and weather.

What's the best mowing height for Waco grass?

For Bermuda grass, 1-2 inches is ideal. St. Augustine should be cut to 2.5-3 inches. We adjust cutting height seasonally for optimal lawn health.

Do you service during summer heat in Waco?

Yes! We start early morning service during hot summer months to protect both your lawn and our crew. We may adjust schedules during extreme heat advisories.

What areas around Waco do you service?

We provide mowing services throughout Waco and surrounding communities including Woodway, Robinson, Bellmead, Hewitt, and Lacy Lakeview.
